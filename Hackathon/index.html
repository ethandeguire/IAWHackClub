The Hackathon

HackIAW is a hackathon being run by the programming club Hack Club at the International Academy West.

The hackathon is open to people with any level of programming expertise, and will be a learning experience for everyone.

In addition to having a great time, learning to code and being active in local events looks great on college applications!
